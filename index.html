The first steps to a healthier mind

  1. Sit comfortably on a chair or a pillow. You may close your eyes.
  2. Focus on your breathing. Notice when you breathe in, breathe out.
  3. If your attention slips, again pay attention to your breathing.
  4. Repeat the above steps, be patient and kind to yourself.
  5. Now you are meditating.

Consistency is key

Try to meditate on a regular basis. Find a place with few disturbances, at a time that suits you. Hopefully you can find time to meditate daily for best results. During a few minutes break is fine!

In addition, keeping your back and neck straight is advised. Sitting with regular crossed legs might not be lenient on your back. Experiment to find what's most comfortable for you!
